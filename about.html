Kalawaan, Pasig

Barangay Kalawaan is one of the vibrant communities in Pasig City, known for its strong sense of unity and progress. It serves as a home to hardworking residents, small businesses, and community-driven initiatives that reflect the true spirit of Bayanihan.

Our Vision

To be a progressive and sustainable barangay that provides quality service and fosters harmony, transparency, and inclusiveness among residents for a better Pasig community.

Our Mission

To empower our citizens through efficient governance, promote peace and order, and implement programs that improve health, education, livelihood, and overall quality of life within Barangay Kalawaan.
